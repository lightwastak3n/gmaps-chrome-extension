Google Maps Scraper

Only works while on Google Maps

Enter a type of business to be scraped.

Write comma separated list of locations (London, Paris, Sydney Canada,...).

Choose a data format. csv will be downloaded and markdown will be displayed in a new tab.

Press Run to get the data. Locations will be searched and lists will be scrolled automatically. It should take 20-40 seconds per location. Scrolling is capped at 40 seconds or until we reach the bottom.
